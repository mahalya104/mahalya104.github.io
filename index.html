01 / SUMMARY

Ahalya is an environmental engineer who moved from green-building consulting into sustainability reporting — the unglamorous, detail-heavy work that keeps a facility's compliance paperwork honest. She spends her days reconciling water-discharge figures, chemical inventories, and stormwater documentation against EPA standards for U.S. sites, then validating it all again before it ships.

Her background in GHG accounting (Scope 1–3) and carbon-credit project review gives her reporting work a sharper edge: she doesn't just format numbers into GRI, BRSR, or TCFD templates, she understands what the numbers are supposed to mean.
